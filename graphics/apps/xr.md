XR (VR, AR, MR)
===============

## APIs

- OpenXR
  - <https://registry.khronos.org/OpenXR/>
- OpenVR
  - <https://github.com/ValveSoftware/openvr>
- ARKit
  - <https://developer.apple.com/augmented-reality/arkit/>
- ARCore
  - <https://developers.google.com/ar/develop>
- Oculus
- PlayStation VR

## Headsets

- Meta Quest Pro
  - 2022
  - standalone / tethered
  - 1800x1920 per-eye
  - supported apis: oculus, openxr
- Meta Quest 2
  - 2020
  - standalone / tethered
  - 1832x1920 per-eye
  - supported apis: oculus, openxr
- VIVE XR Elite
  - 2023
  - standalone / tethered
  - 1920x1920 per-eye
  - supported apis: openxr
- HP Reverb G2
  - 2022
  - tethered
  - 2160x2160 per-eye
  - supported apis: openxr, openvr
- Valve Index
  - 2019
  - tethered
  - 1440x1600 per-eye
  - supported apis: openxr, openvr
- Tethered, aka, PC VR
  - app runs on pc
  - headset is used as an input/output device
  - it can be wired
    - quest link
  - or wireless
    - quest air link
    - alvr

- Godot
  - <https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/tutorials/xr/index.html>
  - supports: openxr, openvr, arkit
- Unity
  - <https://docs.unity3d.com/Manual/XR.html>
  - supports: openxr, oculus, arkit, arcore, playstation

- Unreal
  - <https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US/xr>
