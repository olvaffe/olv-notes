Imagination PowerVR
===================

## History

- Series1 in 1996-1997
  - licensed to NEC
  - DirectX 3.0
- Series2 in 1998-1999
  - licensed to NEC
  - DirectX 6.0
  - used in Dreamcast
- Series3 in 2000-2002
  - licensed to STMicro
  - DirectX 6.0
- Series4 in 2001
  - very successful on mobile
- Series5 in 2005-2010
  - SGX
  - OpenGL ES 2.0
  - OpenGL 2.1 to 3.2
  - Direct3D 9.0c to 10.1
  - Series5XT in 2009-2010
    - multi-core variant
    - used in PlayStation Vita
  - Series5XE in 2014
    - low-end
- Series6 in 2012-2013
  - Rogue
  - Vulkan 1.1
  - OpenGL ES 3.1
  - OpenGL 3.2
  - OpenCL 1.2
  - Direct3D 10.0
  - Series6XE in 2014
    - low-end
    - Direct3D 9.0 L3
  - Series6XT in 2014
    - low-end
    - OpenGL 3.3
- Series7 in 2014
  - still Rogue
  - Vulkan 1.1
  - OpenGL ES 3.1 and Android Extension Pack
  - OpenCL 1.2 embedded profile
  - Direct3D 9.0 L3
  - Series7XE in 2014
    - entry-level
  - Series7XT in 2014
    - multi-core
  - Series7XT Plus in 2016
