- calories
  - 1g fat has 9cal
  - 1g carbs has 4 cal

    1kg

## Diets

- Target
  - recommended when maintaining,
    - carbs: 45-65%
    - protein: 10-35%
    - fat: 20-35%
  - recommended when losing weight
    - carbs: 10-30%
    - protein: 40-50%
    - fat: 30-40%

    - carbs: 40-45%
    - protein: 35-40%
    - fat: 20%

## Exercises

- commute: one-way 3mi, 20min
  - 20cal per 10kg
  - at 100kg, 200cal one-way
- walking at 4km/h
  - 25cal per 10kg per hour
  - at 100kg and with a 600m loop, 37cal per loop
    - a loop takes 9min
- jogging at 8km/h
  - 70cal per 10kg per hour
  - at 100kg and with a 600m loop, 100cal per loop
    - a loop takes 4.5min
