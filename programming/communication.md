## Mail Filters

- incoming mails
  - sent to me
    - from another person
    - from machine
      - need attention
      - ads
  - sent to list
    - monitor closely
    - semi-interesting
  - sent to me and list
- Label each mail
  - to me or mention me -> label
  - to a list -> label
  - has:nouserlabels -> automate labeling next time
- Inbox: sent to me and not ads
  - not to me -> skip inbox
  - to me but are ads -> skip inbox
